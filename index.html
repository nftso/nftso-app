About NFTSO

With the vision of providing seamless integration of NFT verification, we have a built easy to integrate NFT Sign On service that allows NFT project owners to verify the holders of their tokens in just few seconds.

How it Works

Launch NFT Project

Launch your nft tokens in Ethereum or Polygon network and build a community.

Generate NFTSO Code

Provide your project contract and other details to generate NFTSO installation code.

Install NFTSO Code

Wherever you want to allow people to provide the ownership of your tokens, install the code and let NFTSO do the magic.

Use cases

1. Website Gateway

Install NFTSO on your exclusive area of the website which you only want to get accessed by your NFT holders.

2. Exclusive Events

Keep your event ticket sales private and allow only your NFT token holders to purchase with verification.

3. E-commerce

Launch your NFT merch / product store and exclusively make it open and accessible for your NFT holders.

4. Video Access

Launch videos, premium playlists on your website and protect with NFTSO so that only your holders can watch them.

5. Discord Integration

Install NFTSO bot in your server and let your members verify as a holders and unlock other perks in discord.

6. 3rd Party Partnerships

Give your NFT holders an access to your 3rd party partnerships and let them verify as a holders with NFTSO to access the perks.
